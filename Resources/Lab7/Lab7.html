CS 2340 - Lab 7: Database I (25 Points)
Due 9 PM, Monday, November 12, 2012

Create a VB.NET program in MS Visual Studio 2010 with one code module and two form class, FormClassGrid and FormClassIndividual, to access database records.

Supporting files
  • The database file is MyCompany.mdb and it is located at S:\Courses\CSSE\yangq\CS2340\ReadOnly\DataBase. You must copy it to your C:\Temp.
  • All other supporting files are also in the folder.
Code Module
  • You must have a code module with a main sub, and the startup object must be Sub Main (or Module).
  • The module must declare two variables, frmGrid of FormClassGrid and frmIndividual of FormClassIndividual.
  • Sub Main must create objects of the two form classes and assign them to frmGrid and frmIndividual, then start the program with frmGrid.
Class FormClassGrid
  • The form has its control box and its title is "Lab7" followed by your first and last names. The StartPosition is center screen, the Window state is normal, and the FormBorderStyle is Sizable.
  • The initial size is (800, 350) and the minimum size is (520, 320).
  • The form has a DataGridView grdSalesStaff of size (514, 220). It has six columns, and the column header text is "ID", "Last Name", "First Name", "Full Time", "Hire Date" and "Salary", respectively. The width of column ID is 50, column Last Name and First Name 95, column Full Time and Salary 70, column Hire Date 80.
  • The ToolTipText of column ID is "Employee ID" and that of column Salary is "Annual Salary."
  • The values of ID are centered, that of Last Name and First Name are left aligned, that of Hire Date and Salary are right aligned. The Hire Date is in the short date format, and Salary is in Currency format without any decimal digits.
  • There is a label above grdSalesStaff with text "Employees." The AutoSize of the label is true, and the font is MS Sans Serif 14 and underlined.
  • The standard DataGridView binding navigator toolbar, SalesStaffBindingNavigator, is on the top of the form. Its back color is ControlDark. The toolbar has an additional ToolStripButton, mnuRefresh, with ToolTipText "Refresh" and the DisplayStyle is Image. All the standard controls on grdSalesStaff have the default names, such as BindingNavigatorDeleteItem.
  • When the form is displayed, all records of table SalesStaff are listed in the grid, and the controls on grdSalesStaff must work as expected.
  • Right clicking on the DataGridView, a shortcut menu, mnuStripStaff, will be displayed with two menu items: mnuIndividual and mnuExit. The text is Individual and Exit, and the shortcut key is Ctrl+I and Ctrl+X, respectively.
  • Clicking on mnuIndividual will hide the form and show frmIndividual, clicking on Exit will terminate the program.
  • Clicking on the form close button in the form controlbox or pressing Alt+F4 will not close the form and the program continues to run.
  • When the form is resized, including loaded at the beginning, the controls will be placed on the form as follows:
    • Horizontally, both lblTitle and grdSalesStaff will be in the center of the form.
    • Vertically, the top margin is the same as the bottom margin, and the middle margin, the space between lblTitle and grdSalesStaff, is 2/3 of the top margin.
Class FormClassIndividual
  • The form has no control box and its title is "Lab7" followed by your first and last names. The StartPosition is center screen, the Window state is normal, the size is (590, 310) and the FormBorderStyle is Fixed3D.
  • The data for one employee is displayed on the form, and your form must be very similar to that of the sample program.
  • At the top of the form, there is a ToolStrip tolStripIndividual. The ToolStrip is very similar to the standard BindingNavigator, but you are NOT allowed to used the BindingNavigator, and you must setup the ToolStrip yourself.
  • The toolbar has eight ToolStripButtons: btnFirst, btnPrevious, btnNext, btnLast, btnAddNew, btnDelete, btnSave, btnRefresh. The four navigation buttons have the text First, Previous, Next and Last, but the other four buttons display images instead of text.
  • The toolbar has one ToolStripTextBox, txtPosition, to display the position of the current record. The position is 1-based. The textbox is ReadOnly, BackColor is Window, and TextAlign is Center.
  • The toolbar has one ToolStripLabel, lblCount, to display the total count of records. The TextAlign is MiddleCenter.
  • The toolbar has three ToolStripSeparators. Your toolbar must be very similar to that of the sample program, including the image, text and tooltiptext.
  • The toolbar has eight ToolStripButtons, one ToolStripTextbox, one ToolStripLabel, and three ToolStripSeparators. Your toolbar must be very similar to that of the sample program, including the image, text and tooltiptext.
  • The functionality of each item of the toolbar is the same as the corresponding item on the standard binding navigator toolbar.
  • All the navigation buttons must be enabled and disabled correctly all the times.
  • After clicking on btnAddNew, all buttons on the toolstrp should be disabled, except the btnSave and btnDelete. The user can enter data and click btnSave to save the new record to database or click btnDelete to discard the new record. All buttons should work normally after clicking Save or Delete.
  • Right clicking on the textbox for ID, Last Name, or First Name will popup a shortcut menu, mnuStripIndividual, with two menu items: mnuGrid and mnuExit. The text is Grid and Exit, and the shortcut key is Ctrl+G and Ctrl+X, respectively.
  • Clicking on mnuGrid will hide the form and show frmGrid, clicking on mnu`Exit will terminate the program.
