CS2340 - Lab 1 (10 points)
Due 5 PM, Wednesday, September 7, 2016

Create a VB.NET program in MS Visual Studio 2012. The program has one form with two textboxes, two labels, and two buttons.

The Form
  • The name is frmLab1.
  • The title is "Lab1" followed by your name, e.g., "Lab1 - Qi Yang"
  • The size of the form is (500, 300), the FormBorderStyle is Fixed3D.
  • The WindowState is Normal and the StartPosition is CenterScreen.
The Textboxes
  • The name is txtInput and txtSquareRoot.
  • The two textboxes together are centered on the form both vertically and horizontally.
  • The size of both textboxes is (100, 20).
  • The space between the two textboxes is between 50 and 100.
  • Textbox txtSquareRoot is ReadOnly, Backcolor is Window, and TextAlign is right.
The Labels
  • The name is lblInput and lblSquareRoot.
  • The text is "Input" and "Square Root" respectively.
  • Each label is above a textbox and aligned on the left.
The Buttons
  • The name is btnCompute and btnExit.
  • The text is "Compute" and "Exit" respectively, and the two underlined chars are the access key.
  • They are below the textboxes and horizontally centered.
  • No space between the two buttons.
  • Button btnCompute is the AccessButton of the frmLab1 and button btnExit is the CancelButton of frmLab1.
The Click Event of the buttons
  • When clicking on btnCompute
    • a message box will be displayed if the input string does not represent a number, including "2e3" and "2E3":
      • title: "Lab 1"
      • text "Incorrect Input!" and "Enter Again!" on two lines
      • icon: Exclamation
      • button: OK
      • The focus will go to txtInput after clicking OK of the message box.
    • a message box will be displayed if the input string represents a negative number:
      • title: "Lab 1"
      • text "Negative Input!" and "Enter Again!" on two lines
      • icon: Exclamation
      • button: OK
      • The focus will go to txtInput after clicking OK of the message box.
    • otherwise, the square root of the input number will be displayed in txtSquareRoot with two decimal digits.
  • Clicking on btnExit will terminate the program.
